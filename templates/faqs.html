FAQs

Q1. How do I contact your office team?
A. Please send us an email at Contact Us.
Q2. What payment methods can I use?
A. We support cash payment, card payment and Apple pay.
Q3. Will the dishes in the restaurant change?
A. We will update the menu from time to time, so you can enjoy special dishes in special seasons.
Q4. Is there any discount for dining in the restaurant?
A. Join the Friends Club can enjoy a 10% discount on dining.
Q5. How to join our restaurant friends club?
A. At checkout, you can apply to join the Friends Club.
Q6. What are the benefits of joining the Friends Club?
A. Get points after eating, reach different levels and enjoy different free dishes.
Q7. Are there any special activities?
A. Couples can enjoy a 60% discount when dining on Valentine's Day.
Q8. Do I need to make an appointment in advance?
A. Yes, but not necessary (you can reserve seats by phone or email).
Q9. How can I report personal property lost or found in the restaurant?
A. If an item is found in store it will be handed into our Office Team on 3 for safe storage. Should you lose anything whilst in store, please contact Office Team by E-mail or Phone.
